Our Flavors

Sweet Tangy BBQ

The original recipe of our Grandfather. This sweet Texas style BBQ made from all natural ingredients will knock off any of the competitions BBQ due to its sweet flavor and rich flavoring. This recipe has never been touched and will be the old classic of Dads BBQ.

Habanero Devil Fire

This BBQ will be sure to knock you back a few as it is our spicy BBQ and will fore sure have you sweating. This BBq is mixed with 10 different spices and peppers that make this our patented spicy BBQ. Voted 2nd most hottest BBQ since 2014 in all of Texas and we plan to keep making our recipe hotter

Alabama Zesty White BBQ

Our Alabama Zesty White BBQ was inspired in 1974 in Alabama by our grandfather. THis BBQ is a cool and tangy BBQ that is extremely unique, but well liked throughout restaurants all over America. This BBQ is something for people who love a sweet tangy flavor and cool ranch feeling. This one is perfect for any brisket or choice of meat you are feeling to use.

North Carolina Reaper

Our North Carolina Reaper is our unique blend of ghost peppers mixed with herbs and spices. This BBQ is mixed with specific herbs and spices that are native to North Carolina to give it that authentic North carolina feeling. This one will give you enough and perfect blend between sweet and spicy.The perfect blend for people who want the just right amount of kick to it and still want that sweet taste.
